Smart Steels: Building Dreams from the Ground Up

Founded in 2020 by Rajendrakumar PM, Smart Steels started as a small venture in Edapal, Ponani, Kerala, with a big vision. After years of working as a Document Controller at Qatar Petroleum, Rajendrakumar decided to leave his secure job and return to his roots, driven by the desire to contribute to his community. Recognizing the growing demand for quality construction materials in the region, Rajendrakumar launched Smart Steels, offering essential products like TMT steel bars, cement, roofing sheets, and more. Despite the challenges of starting a business during uncertain times, his dedication and experience helped Smart Steels thrive. Today, Smart Steels is known for its commitment to quality and customer service. From supplying top-notch building materials to offering expert advice, we have become a trusted partner for contractors and homeowners alike. With a focus on reliability and integrity, Smart Steels is helping build the future, one project at a time.
